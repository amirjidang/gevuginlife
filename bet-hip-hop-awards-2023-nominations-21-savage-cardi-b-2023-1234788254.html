21 Savage, Cardi B And More

August 23, 2024
post

BET has announced its nominations for the 2023 BET Hip-Hop Awards. Selected by a voting academy of music industry insiders, 21 Savage and Cardi B lead with 12 nominations each. The two rhymers are followed by Drake, who earned nine total nominations.

Burna Boy and DJ Khaled round out the top five with seven nods each. J. Cole counts six nominations while Coi Leray, GloRilla, and Jay-Z are tied with five each. Other notable nominations include Lil Uzi Vert, who received four nominations, and DaBaby, Kendrick Lamar, Latto, Lil Durk, Megan Thee Stallion, and Metro Boomin, all earning three nominations.

The 2023 BET Hip-Hop Awards are set to tape live in Atlanta on Oct. 3 and air the following week.

“This year’s BET Hip Hop Awards will mark the culmination of our year-long celebration of Hip-Hop’s storied creation and the art form’s sonic and cultural expansion,” expressed Connie Orlando, EVP Specials, Music Programming & Music Strategy for BET.

“We are honored by this year’s roster of nominees as they continue to carry the creativity and diversity of Hip-Hop into the future. We congratulate each nominee on their accomplishments as they take Hip-Hop artistry to new heights.”
