These 15 Celebs Reveal Who They Shared Their First Kiss With

First is always special, whether it's first love, first crush, first hug, or first kiss. No matter how old we are, the first kiss is remembered fondly since it brings back innocent memories from the past and fills us with nostalgia.

The first kiss also takes us down the memory lane. Our minds are filled with memories of where our first kisses happened, how they happened, and with whom they were shared. Some also associate their first kisses with funny incidents or surprising facts.

Here are some intriguing facts and amusing stories that celebrities shared about their first kisses. You'll love learning about the first kisses of these famous people. You may be able to relate to some of these "first kiss" stories.

15 Mila Kunis

Mila Kunis is hugely fortunate to have shared her first kiss with none other than Hollywood heartthrob and current husband, Ashton Kutcher. The two worked together for the first time on That 70’s Show. That is the time when they shared their first kiss, which was also a first for Mila Kunis.

14 Carly Rae Jepsen

Interestingly, singer Carly Rae Jepsen has an album titled, "This Kiss". In an interview, she revealed that she had her first kiss with a jazz singer known as Brenden Call. She described the guy as sweet and gentle, adding that the two are still very much in touch on social media.

13 Zac Efron

According to Hollywoodlife, Zac's first onscreen kiss was with Kay Panabaker, and Zac wanted her to feel comfortable. Unfortunately, he ate a bunch of seafood for lunch, and Kay wasn't wild about his seafood breath. Zac learned from that mistake. Plenty of people still want to kiss him.

12 Charlize Theron

We can’t imagine Theron’s first kiss being anything but steamy and passionate. However, it was far from it. Charlize said in an interview that her first kiss was awkward - she was in her pre-teens. What made it awkward was the fact she wore braces at the time. She stole a kiss in the backyard.

11 Reese Witherspoon

The female actor remembers her first kiss vividly and says that the first time she locked lips, it was with a boy called Graham. She said that Graham was a great kisser and she enjoyed kissing him. Her first kiss in movies, however, was with Jason London in the film, Man on the Moon.

10 Ed Sheeran

Ed Sheeran’s music is exceptionally romantic, to say the least. To say that Ed's first kiss was awkward is an understatement. He tried to use his tongue just like he saw actors do in the movies. He said that his first kiss reminded him of a kiss that Pierce Brosnan enjoyed in one of his films.

9 Selena Gomez

The first kiss never happened offscreen. It was filmed for fans to watch. Her first kiss was on an episode of the TV show, The Suite Life of Zack and Cody. She said that she had no clue how to kiss back then because she was very young and innocent. She was only twelve.

8 Leonardo DiCaprio

The actor has a funny and disgusting story to share about his first kiss. It was with a girl who left a ton of saliva in his mouth. He had the audience laughing when he added that, after the kiss was over, and the walked away, he quickly spit out all the saliva from his mouth.

7 Lucy Liu

Liu shared that her first kiss was with someone she had a crush on. She said that the boy leaned in and kissed her after she watched a movie with him. She calls the kiss awkward as she did not even know back then what French kissing meant and so it took her by surprise.

6 Justin Bieber

Pop sensation Justin Bieber’s first kiss was equally awkward. The first time he locked his lips with a girl was atop the mountain, and both of them were extremely cold when it happened. He says that he was as young as 11 or 12 at the time of his first kiss.

5 Shailene Woodley

Shailene calls herself a late bloomer and shared that, at the age of fourteen, she still found herself drawn to Barbies. Her first kiss happened at the age of fifteen and a half. She said that the person she shared her first kiss with had big and beautiful lips and she didn’t know how to kiss.

4 Tyler Posey

It is interesting to note that Tyler had his first kiss with singer and actor, Miley Cyrus. He revealed that they were both only 19 when they first shared the kiss, which blossomed into a two-year-long relationship. Their relationship was followed by a breakup, after which they continued to be friends.

3 Leighton Meester

The female actor said in an interview that her first kiss astounded her and she did not know what to do after. She was sixteen and kissed a guy from her school, and the kiss happened right after he walked her home and they watched a movie together at his place.

She was extremely nervous when it happened, and was so scared after they kissed that she just wanted to go home as soon as possible.

2 Taylor Swift

Swift’s dating life has always been interesting, and the story behind her first kiss is equally intriguing. The singer revealed that she shared her first kiss with a guy when she was just fifteen years old. The kiss turned into a year-long relationship.
