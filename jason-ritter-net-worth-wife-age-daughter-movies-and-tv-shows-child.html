Jason Morgan Ritter is an American actor and producer.

Jason Ritter is also a grandson of actors Tex Ritter (who died 6 years before Jason was born) and Dorothy Fay.

Jason Ritter Age

Jason Ritter was born on February 17, 1980 in Los Angeles, California, to actors Nancy Morgan and John Ritter.

Jason Ritter Movies and TV Shows

Jason Ritter is known for his roles as Kevin Girardi in the television series Joan of Arcadia, Ethan Haas in The Class, Sean Walker in the NBC series The Event, Dipper Pines in Gravity Falls, and Pat Rollins in Raising Dion.

In 2003, Jason Ritter had a major role in the slasher/horror film Freddy vs. Jason as Will Rollins.

Jason Ritter also appeared in Raise Your Voice and Happy Endings.

Jason Ritter portrayed Jeb Bush in the Oliver Stone film W.

In 2007, Jason Ritter also voiced substitute teacher, Mr. Fisk, in an episode of All Grown Up!, the grown up version of Rugrats, a teacher whom Angelica falls in love with.

In 2008, Jason Ritter released Good Dick, a movie which he produced and starred in along with his long-term girlfriend at the time, Marianna Palka (with whom he began a relationship after meeting in Queens in October 1999), who also wrote and directed the film.

Jason Ritter Wife

Jason Ritter married actress Melanie Lynskey after four years of dating.

Jason Ritter Child

Jason Ritter and Melanie Lynskey had their first child, a daughter, in December 2018.
