The Truth About Samantha Ronson And Lindsay Lohan's Relationship

It seems like Lindsay Lohan has tried to have quite a few comebacks and it's safe to say that things went downhill for the star a while ago.

While there's quite a lot of information out there about Lohan and Wilmer Valderrama's romance, no one knows a lot about the time when Lohan was dating Samantha Ronson. The pair was together from 2008 until 2009, so it was short-lived but it was also very dramatic. Let's take a look at their love story.

Good Friends

Lohan has dated a lot of guys away from the public eye but when she was with Samantha Ronson, they were definitely in the limelight, as they weren't hiding their romance.

Sometimes a friendship will turn into a romance, and other times, both people will feel that they're better off in a platonic relationship than anything else. It sounds like that might have happened between Lindsay Lohan and her ex-girlfriend.

According to the actress, Lindsay Lohan and Samantha Roson were good friends. People.com says that Lohan was a guest on The Howard Stern Show in 2019 and she said, “I talk about that on the show," meaning her MTV reality series Lindsay Lohan's Beach Club. The actress continued, “We’re friends. We were more friends than anything."

Ronson And Lohan's Love Story

Besides saying that they were better off as friends, Lohan has also used the word "toxic" to describe the way that things were between her and her ex-girlfriend. According to Us Weekly, Lohan said back in 2012, "I needed to love myself before I could be with anyone. And I was going through a lot. Two toxic people cannot be together. End of story.”

According to Intomore.com, Lohan called the love story between her and Ronson "serious" in 2012 and said they were "in love." A few years before, in 2008, she said she wasn't interested in classifying herself. She said, “I don’t want to classify myself. First of all, you never know what’s going to happen tomorrow, in a month, a year from now, five years from now.”

Ranker.com says that Lohan proudly said she was with Ronson. She was quoted saying, "I was bold enough to say, 'Yeah, I like a girl. And?'"

It definitely sounds like the stars had quite a rocky time together, and even after they broke up, it continued. Lohan lived right next to Ronson once they had split up. According to Ranker.com, after the couple went their separate ways in April 2009, Lohan started living in a home right next to her ex-girlfriend once she was finished with rehab. As a source explained, “Samantha walked out of the building next door. She was shaking her head and looking disgusted. She kept saying, ‘I didn't plan it this way.’”

It also sounds like the pressures of fame might have made it tough for Lohan and Ronson to maintain their relationship. When she was interviewed by Harper's Bazaar in 2008, Lohan talked around the romance without really getting too specific, but what she did say was pretty telling. Lohan said, "I feel like it jinxes it. It's hard. The second I start talking about whomever I'm seeing, a month or two later it's failed."

The actress continued, "I think it's pretty obvious who I'm seeing. I think it's no shock to anyone that it's been going on for quite some time. ... She's a wonderful person and I love her very much."

Lohan's Exes

Lindsay Lohan has definitely had a fairly active love life over the years, and fans of the actress will recall that she has been linked to many famous people.

According to Us Weekly, in 2016 Lohan was engaged to Egor Tarabasov but that didn't end up going anywhere. Lohan and Aaron Carter were a couple in 2003, she started dating Wilmer Valderrama in 2004, and she was linked to Stavros Niarchos in 2006.

Ronson And Lohan Today

Ronson has recently been in the news as she has spoken about the situation at The Ellen DeGeneres Show. According to The Sun, she said, "Unpopular opinion: I’ve worked for @TheEllenShow as a DJ, for her parties, for years and she has ALWAYS been respectful and kind to me."

As for Lohan, she is getting ready to see her mom get married, according to Vanity Fair. Jesse Nadler and Dina Lohan are tying the knot soon and Lohan is going to be the maid of honor.
