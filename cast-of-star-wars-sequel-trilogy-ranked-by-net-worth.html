The Cast Of The Star Wars Sequel Trilogy Ranked By Net Worth

August 30, 2024
post

The Star Wars franchise is undoubtedly one of the most popular movie series of all time. Not only does each film generate huge sums of money but there is also a wide range of merchandise, books, comics, games, and TV shows based on the property. With this mind, it's no wonder the franchise has made most of its stars wealthy people. Whether it is through lucrative contracts, image rights, or just a steady stream of work, Star Wars has been very kind to many actors.

The latest sequel trilogy is no different in that respect. The new films brought back old favorites and introduced the world to a collection of relatively unknown faces. The likes of Harrison Ford and Mark Hamill showed up on the screen, next to the talented actor, John Boyega, and Daisy Ridley. So, just how do all of these actors stack up next to each other when it comes to net worth? Let’s find out below.

13 Harrison Ford Is The Biggest Earner, With A Net Worth Of $230 Million

Out of all of the Star Wars actors, Hollywood legend, Harrison Ford, is probably the one who had the most successful career. It should come as little surprise, then, that the superstar is the person who has amassed the most wealth. Over the course of his impressive career, the actor has played Han Solo, Indiana Jones, Jack Ryan, and many other memorable characters.

12 Andy Serkis Has A Surprising Net Worth Of $28 Million

Andy Serkis has become well-known for his roles in movies. He's renowned for providing 'motion capture' performances. For example, he took on the role of Gollum in Lord of the Rings and the giant ape in King Kong. Serkis has also had a career as a director and appeared in hit series' like the MCU. All of this has helped him build a net worth of $28 million.

11 Carrie Fisher’s Net Worth Was $25 Million Before Her Passing

Before her tragic death, Carrie Fisher was a huge success. Not only had she been a successful actor but she was also a highly-acclaimed writer and script doctor. Thanks to the sales of her books and the rest of her work, she was able to amass wealth totaling about $25 million.

10 Mark Hamill’s Net Worth Is In The Region Of $18 Million

Mark Hamill might have been the main character in the original Star Wars movies but that didn’t mean he had the most success after those films. However, he's still had a long career as an actor and voiceover artist. Appearing in a wide array of movies, TV shows, and video games, he is now worth around $18 million.

9 Palpatine Actor, Ian McDiarmid, Is Worth $10 Million

Ian McDiarmid is an actor who has had a distinguished career over the last few decades. He first got his start in the Star Wars franchise, in the original trilogy, as Emperor Palpatine. He has since reprised that role in the prequel trilogy, before making an appearance in The Rise of Skywalker in 2019. His net worth is believed to be $10 million.

8 Oscar Isaac Is Believed To Be Worth $10 Million

Oscar Isaac plays the starfighter, Poe Dameron, in the Star Wars sequel trilogy. Unlike some of the other actors from the new films, he was not a completely unfamiliar face in Hollywood when he was cast. That is one of the reasons why his net worth is higher than many of his co-stars in the series.

7 Adam Driver Is Worth An Approximate $8 Million

Although many people will only know Adam Driver for playing Kylo Ren in Star Wars, he has had a number of other high-profile roles in movies, and some of those films have even been nominated for Academy Awards. This is what has helped him build a net worth of $8 million. He's one of the most successful of the new cast members.

6 Anthony Daniels Has Made $8 Million Playing C-3PO

Anthony Daniels is another actor who has appeared in the Star Wars franchise since the very beginning. Playing the iconic role of C-3PO, the droid has had a part in all of the main movies in the franchise. Thanks to that, and his other roles in Hollywood and television, the actor is believed to be worth $8 million.

5 Warwick Davis’s Net Worth Is Around $8 Million

Warwick Davis has become strongly associated with the Star Wars franchise since he first appeared in Return of the Jedi. After that, he became a regular fixture in the movies and had minor roles in the sequel trilogy. Throughout his career as an actor and television presenter, he has amassed around $8 million.

4 Domhnall Gleeson (General Hux) Has A Net Worth Of $7 Million

Domhnall Gleeson plays the part of General Hux in the Star Wars sequel trilogy. After the death of Supreme Leader Snoke, he became the main villain for a short amount of time. Before that, he appeared in the Harry Potter franchise as Bill Weasley. He has had a number of other roles, giving him a net worth of $7 million.

3 Daisy Ridley’s Estimated Wealth Is $6 Million

Out of all the new characters introduced in the Star Wars sequel trilogy, Daisy Ridley arguably had the biggest role. She played the part of Rey in the films, and she was the focus of the story. Before landing that part, she had only appeared in a small number of short films. So far, she's amassed $6 million.

2 John Boyega Has A Net Worth Of $6 Million

John Boyega had something of a rocky start in Star Wars after he scored the role of one of the new main characters. This was largely the result of racism from some sections of the community. Of course, that didn’t stop him from becoming a rousing success in the role, and he is now believed to be worth around $6 million.

1 Kelly Marie Tran (Rose) Is Worth $3 Million

Kelly Marie Tran appeared in the sequel trilogy as Rose. She struck up a relationship with Finn. Before appearing in Star Wars, she had roles in the likes of XOXO and Hot Girls on the Beach. Out of all of the cast from the new films, she seems to have the lowest net worth, which stands at just $3 million.
