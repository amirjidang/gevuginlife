Killian Dain Bids Farewell To Sanity

It was revealed last night that Sanity leader Eric Young will be jumping over to the RAW brand in the Superstar Shake Up, and Killian Dain took to Twitter to wish both him and Alexander Wolfe the best of luck as they all seem to be moving on from the faction. It’s unknown as to if Wolfe will be staying or leaving the SmackDown brand, but this looks to be a pretty clear indication that Sanity is indeed no more.

Dasha Fuentes Thanks WWE For Opportunity

Dasha Fuentes was recently let go by WWE but there certainly appears to be no hard feelings between the two parties as Fuentes took to Instagram to share a video thanking WWE for the five years she received as an employee of the company:

I can’t thank you all of enough for the past 5 years it has truly been an honor and privilege to have been able to work for @wwe!!! To all of my fans throughout the years and coworkers who have reached out to me it truly means a lot!!! Although my journey with WWE has come to an end it’s just the beginning!!! I most definitely am not saying goodbye, but rather I’ll see y’all around!!!!
