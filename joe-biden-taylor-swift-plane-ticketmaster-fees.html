Joe Biden Is Passing A Lot To Stop The Tracking Of Taylor Swift's Plane, While He's Also Helping Swifties Out By Eliminating Unnecessary Fees

Without a doubt, getting an endorsement from Taylor Swift might be a tipping point for Joe Biden. For the time being, it seems like Biden is doing his very best to keep Swift content. In the latest act, Biden signed off on a bill that makes it impossible to track Taylor Swift's private jet.

Yahoo Entertainment wrote, "An amendment in the Federal Aviation Administration re-authorization bill that was passed last week will allow private aircraft owners to anonymize their registration information. President Joe Biden signed the FAA bill into law on May 16th, after it passed in the Senate 88-4 and the House 387 to 26."

Tracking jets has been made possible due to aircraft owners being forced to register where they are. That seems to be changing, and it is happening at the perfect time for Swift, who is facing heat over the constant use of her jet.

Swifties got some positive news of their own as well. In what is deemed as an attempt to win more votes, Biden is removing junk fees, especially with the likes of TicketMaster, as costs to watch Taylor Swift's shows have grown to be unaffordable for some families.

NPR wrote, "[Biden's] not saying junk fees are bad — period," the official said. "He's using it to tell the broader story about competition and the need to make sure there's a fair and level playing field for businesses, to make sure that people understand what they're getting upfront."

Biden himself also commented on the matter. He said, "The American people are tired of being played for suckers. No matter how much money you make, no matter how rich or poor or middle class you are, you don't like being taken advantage of."

Some major moves by Biden. But still, Swift hasn't formally supported the current President.

Taylor Swift Still Hasn't Endorsed Joe Biden Despite Being Urged To Do So By Others

At the moment, Taylor Swift hasn't supported any President, and this really shouldn't come as a surprise. Given her diverse fanbase, it might be in Tay's best interest to lie low on the matter. However, she's feeling the pressure from others. Hollywood director Rob Reiner urged Taylor to support Biden.

He wrote on X, "I'm in the midst of shooting a sequel to This is Spinal Tap. As much as I'd like Taylor Swift to do a cameo in the film, I'd give anything for her to endorse Joe Biden. She would virtually single-handedly save American Democracy."

It should be noted that Swift did support Biden during his 2020 run, while she didn't do the same for Donald Trump. Still, at the moment, she hasn't outright supported the current President. She seems to be occupied with her current tour taking over Europe.
