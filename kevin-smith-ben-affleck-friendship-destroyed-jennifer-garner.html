Are Kevin Smith And Ben Affleck Still Friends?

Despite their estrangement, Ben Affleck and Kevin Smith found their way back to each other. Unsurprisingly, once the Alias actress was no longer controlling who Aflleck could be friends with, he and Smith rekindled their friendship. It started when the Batman actor began making cameos again in his friend’s films. In 2019, Affleck returned to acting with his friend by making a cameo in Jay and Silent Bob Reboot.

“I actually felt like Reboot was the new beginning of a beautiful friendship. It was like, ‘Oh, we’re back. Now I can reach out to Ben anytime.”

Ben Affleck also appeared in Kevin Smith’s 2022 film, Clerks III, and reportedly spent time with the director on set. “We spent more time bullsh**ting than we did actually making the movie. But I was glad; it just felt like things were right.”

Smith also attended Ben Affleck’s wedding with Jennifer Lopez in Georgia, so their friendship will likely go on as it did before. Kevin Smith said he cried during the ceremony. "I'm telling you this wedding would have made even the most hard-core cynic believe in true love."
