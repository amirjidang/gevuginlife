Pat Robertson is a renowned American media personality, TV evangelist, political commentator, and former Republican presidential candidate.

Pat Robertson was born on March 22, 1930, in Lexington, Virginia, in the United States of America as the son of Absalom Willis Robertson, a former conservative Democratic Senator, and his wife Gladys Churchill.

Robertson advocates a conservative Christian ideology and is known widely for his past activities in Republican party politics.

He graduated from Washington and Lee University, then almost coming off the top of his class at Yale Law School in 1955 but failed the New York bar exam.

According to Robertson, failing the bar cost Robertson opportunities at post-graduate employment, and in the ensuing months of disappointment, embarrassment, and unemployment, he became a born-again Christian and began a career as a minister.

Pat Robertson Son

The minister of God has four children in all. Highly recognized among them is his son, Gordon Perry Robertson who is the CEO of the Christian Broadcasting Network.

Pat Robertson Wife

Robertson has since the year 1954 been married to his wife and the mother of his children, Dede Elmer.

Pat Robertson Net Worth

Being a politician and a businessman of a high order, Robertson has some good wealth for his family. He has an estimated net worth of $100 million.
