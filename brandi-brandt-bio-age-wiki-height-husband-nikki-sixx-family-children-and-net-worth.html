Brandi Brandt Biography

Brandi Brandt is an American model and actress, who was Playboy’s Playmate of the Month for October 1987. She was born on November 2, 1968, in Santa Clara, California to parents Mike Brandt and drummer, percussionist, and vocalist, Brie Howard-Darling (née Berry). Her parents divorced in the year 1972 and she split her time with her mother in Los Angeles, California, and her father in Sacramento, California.

When Brandi was 12 years old, she met comedic actor Jon Lovitz, who at the time was in a Groundlings class with American Girls bandmate Hillary Matthews. Brandi and Lovitz became friends.

    Brandi Brandt Playboy

    At age 18, Brandi appeared as the Playmate of the Month and cover girl for the October 1987 Back To Campus issue of Playboy magazine. Brandi also appeared in Playboy Blondes, Brunettes, Redhead’s magazine, and on the cover with Debi Nicole Johnson and Sandra Wild.

    Brandi was the cover model for Playboy’s August 1989 Women Of Wall Street issue and appeared on the cover of the March 1990 issue with Donald Trump. An autographed copy of Playboy with President Donald Trump and Brandi on the cover sold at auction for $4,900 by Goldin Auctions as well as has Trump’s recognizable signature scrawled across its cover. The minimum bid was $100, and there were 33 bids on Lot #1338.

    Brandi Brandt Arrest

    Brandi was involved with a cocaine-trafficking syndicate importing cocaine into Australia between July and December 2007. On November 15, 2013, she was extradited from Los Angeles, California, to Sydney, Australia, to face criminal charges in a Sydney court. In April 2014, she pleaded guilty to a charge of conspiring to import drugs. On August 29, 2014, Brandt was sentenced to up to six years in prison. Brandt became eligible for parole in November 2016.
