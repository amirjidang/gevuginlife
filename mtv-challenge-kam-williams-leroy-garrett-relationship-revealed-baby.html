10 Kam Williams And Leroy Garrett Didn't Hit It Off Right Away

It wasn't necessarily love at first sight for this couple. They didn't click perfectly right away, but it was tough to deny that there was definitely an attraction between them, on some level. Leroy played on MTV's The Challenge for several years before he met Kam, but when she joined 2018's The Vendettas, the two crossed paths. Interestingly, it wasn't until a full three seasons later, when they were in War Of The World II that fans noticed there was something happening between them.

9 They First Entered Into A Very Casual Relationship

8 There Was A Break-up, And Then A Make-up

Fans began really paying attention to the development of Kam and Leroy's relationship, and at some point, between the War of the Worlds in 2019 and Double Agents, the two sent off some serious on-again, off-again vibes. 7 Deep Romance Regrets

Much like in other relationships, there were some moments between Kam Williams and Leroy Garrett that were full of regrets. 6 In 2020, Things Got Serious For Kam Williams And Leroy Garrett

Despite the ambiguity in that phase of their relationship, Leroy and Kam seemed to find their way back to one another again, and Leroy went on to say that his best game was played in season 36, when he and Kam had formed a secret alliance. They made sure to play on different teams to avoid being targeted, and their coy move got them both all the way to the finals. They were clearly working together in more ways than one, as their personal life began to spark up again.

5 They Started A New Chapter

In February of 2020, Kam Williams stunned fans with an Instagram post that revealed some huge news. Her social media message announced that she and Leroy Garrett had decided to move in with one another. The two had signed a lease and were on their way to move to Houston. They both seemed to be very excited about embarking on the next chapter in their lives, with Kam quoted as saying, “I’m so excited to start this journey with you, especially since it’s something new for the both of us. I didn’t know my life would’ve changed like this when I met you 4 years ago & I’m happy that it did... to us and our new life.”

4 They Both Hit A High Note On 'The Challenge'

One of the most interesting aspects of their relationship is the fact that in spite of intertwining a hugely competitive game on The Challenge and a complex dating relationship, both Kam Williams and Leroy Garrett managed to hit a high note on the reality TV show. The bond and alliance they formed got them each a Gold Skull, and they were able to both see success and recognition for all of their efforts.

Kam went on to publicly throw her support behind Leroy's decision and professed how incredibly happy she was to see him in pursuit of his dreams with such focus.

She's a fierce competitor, and many fans hope for her return, however, she hasn't made a firm announcement on whether or not she'd consider a comeback.
