Redmond O'Neal, 26, Arrested for Heroin Possession

August 11, 2024
post

Redmond O'Neal is in trouble with the law yet again.

The 26-year-old son of Ryan O'Neal and the late Farrah Fawcett was pulled over in Santa Monica early Tuesday morning for running a red light. During the traffic stop, officers discovered heroin in O'Neal's car.

O'Neal was booked for the red light infraction, violating felony drug probation and possession of a controlled substance, Santa Monica Police Sgt. Richard Lewis told The Los Angeles Times.

O'Neal — who was not under the influence at the time of his arrest — was booked and held without bail pending a Thursday court appearance. Because he was on felony probation, O'Neal could be sent back to prison.
