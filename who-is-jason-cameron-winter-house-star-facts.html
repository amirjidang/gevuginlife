Model Jason Cameron join the cast of Winter House on Bravo in 2011. The spin-off took place at a wintry lodge in Stowe, Vermont, where the cast stayed together for several weeks. The new cast members were accompanied by popular Bravo stars from Southern Charm and Summer House.

According to Jason's bio on BravoTV, he was born and raised in Rochester, New York. He works as a model and now lives in New York City. Just one look at his Instagram indicates he is having the time of his life living and working in the city. So, who is Jason Cameron, what does he do for a living, and will he be back in season 2 of Winter House?

6 Jason Cameron Dated Lindsey Hubbard

Lindsay Hubbard and Jason Cameron, both 35, met while filming Winter House, the Vermont spinoff reality show of Summer House, in February 2021. “Jason’s hot as f—k and he can cook! He’s just so calming and sweet and sexy. Are you kidding me, those abs? Jesus, and the below the abs? Oh, my God, forget it,” Lindsay gushed during the series. “This guy’s been here this whole time, and I was over here distracted by idiots.”

She also told her costar Julia McGuire, “I lay in bed with him, and I’m just like, is this f—king for real? I think he’s so sweet, he’s so genuine and just wants to make sure that’s actually the case. I wanna like, bring him home to my apartment back in the city.”

Lindsay Hubbard revealed she suffered a miscarriage just hours after finding out she was pregnant and expecting a child with Jason Cameron. “Jason was absolutely wonderful, but I also just needed to get through it on my own,” she said. Hubbard has since struck up a relationship with her co-star Carl Radke.

5 What Jason Cameron Does For Work

Jason Cameron is currently signed to One Management in New York and has modeled for brands like Johnston & Murphy and Perry Ellis. Jason also appeared in an ad for Color & Co by L'Oreal, an at-home hair dye system for men.

Alongside modeling, Cameron works for the non-profit Glam 4 Good, which provides clothing, personal care items and more to low-income youth in NYC. Cameron told Page Six that he was “helping open a bakery at the local farmers market” last summer, just a mere 25 minutes from the Sag Harbor house where the cast lived when filming Summer House.

4 Jason Cameron's Mother

Jason Cameron lost his mother when he was a teenager and his grandmother stepped in to raise him. His grandmother died at the age of 99 in 2021. His cast bio expresses, “Jason lives every day with the intention of making his angels [mother and grandmother] proud.”

While it’s unclear how close he was to his dad growing up, Jason gave him a sweet shout-out on Father’s Day in June 2021.

“You are half of the fashion inspiration, half of the decent looks but ALL in when it comes to being a Father. Can’t thank you enough for understanding, accepting but always challenging my hard-headed ways and being there while I found MY path in life,” he wrote on Instagram. “Sorry couldn’t be there with ya. Here’s a photo dump to you and to more photos of us in the near future! Happy Father's Day, I love you.”

3 Why Jason Cameron Doesn't Appear On 'Summer House'

The Winter House star made very few appearances in the recent season of Summer House. While the model was not an official castmate for Summer House season 6, his name was mentioned several times in the season 6 premiere. At the first group dinner, Lindsay Hubbard confirmed that Jason Cameron was supposed to be at the house during the first weekend in the Hamptons. "I told him not to come," Lindsay told her co-star, Paige DeSorbo.

Later on in the episode, Lindsay opened up to Carl Radke about her pregnancy loss and how that led to a shift in her romance with Jason. He was at the Hamptons around this time, just a different period.

“I was out there every weekend. I was just busy doing my own thing,” Jason exclusively told Page Six at the Urban Skin Rx launch party. “So, you know, everyone’s still in touch, and we’re all still cordial as hell." He also shared that the cast still has a group chat they use, and he keeps in touch with almost everyone from Winter House season 1.

2 Jason Cameron Likes To Party

When he is not working, Jason likes to party. His cast bio states that he is an extrovert who has an active social calendar that includes a lot of tequila. His Instagram also shows him partying and traveling.

1 'Winter House' Season 2 Is Coming Soon

Andrea Denver confirmed in February that Bravo would be filming a second season of Winter House. During an appearance on The Dipp’s “Morgan’s Pop Talks” podcast, he revealed that the gang would be returning to Vermont.

Jason Cameron teased his return to Vermont, exclusively telling Us in April 2022, fans are “going to have to wait and see” how his comeback plays out. Carl and Lindsay's romance will be shown, so we expect to see some drama between the trio.
