It's never easy being one of eight children, and growing up on a reality show makes that even tougher. It's challenging for siblings to not feel like they're competing for their parents' attention. In the case of the kids from the TLC reality series Jon And Kate Plus 8, they were thrown into the limelight while they were super young and unable to make decisions about appearing on television.

Since Jon and Kate Plus 8 went off the air, the Gosselin siblings have made their own choices about whether or not they want to live private lives. Collin Gosselin opened up about his tough life and fans have caught up with Madelyn Gosselin's current reality. Fans don't hear that much from Leah Hope Gosselin, though. Here's what she is doing today.

What Has Leah Hope Gosselin Done Since Jon And Kate Plus 8?

Jon and Kate Gosselin remain in the public eye, even though they are now divorced and no longer starring on a reality TV show. People are still endlessly curious about their lives and their connection to their eight kids. There are many questions like whether Jon Gosselin pays child support.

Some of Jon and Kate Gosselin's kids have been more public than others. Leah Hope Gosselin is one of the more private members of the family. She is one of Kate and Jon's sextuplets along with Joel Kevin Gosselin, Collin Thomas Gosselin, Aaden Jonathan Gosselin, Alexis Faith Gosselin, and Hannah Joy Gosselin.

While few details are known about Leah, some sources have reported on what her living situation was like while she attended high school.

According to People, Kate and Jon Gosselin had a custody agreement that meant that Alexis, Joel, Leah, and Aaden lived with Kate before they turned 18. Collin and Hannah were living with Jon. The New York Post reported in 2022 that Kate had a house in North Carolina.

It doesn't sound like Jon and Leah are part of each other's lives. While neither has commented on what their bond looks like now, Jon made some comments to People that suggest that he has a relationship with Collin and Hannah, but that's about it. He said in June 2023, "I had eight graduations this year. I only attended one. The only kids I see and talk to are Hannah and Collin. So I've been pretty much estranged from Mady and Cara for nine years."

Since Leah Hope Gosselin is 19 years old, she doesn't have to live with her mother Kate anymore. From Leah Hope Gosselin's TikTok channel, it's clear that she is now a college student.

In late September 2023, Leah posted a TikTok of her room at college. She wrote "my new home" in the caption and added the hashtag "dorm." She doesn't seem to have shared where she is attending school, and she hasn't explained what types of classes she is taking, either. She might not want people to know.

She does seem to be enjoying her time at school, though. In August 2023, she posted a TikTok of what she has been up to. She wrote in the caption "college has my heart" and shared that she got Starbucks in between classes and was having lunch with pals that she had made. She said, "The dinner choices were questionable but got food anyway," which many college students can relate to.

From Leah's TikTok account, fans can also see that she loves to cook and bake. She has shared videos for kitchen sink cookies, pistachio baklava, cupcakes, and fruit flan. She definitely has the support of some of her siblings. In February 2023, Hannah Gosselin commented, "how did i not know you had a baking page till now!!" In March 2023, when Leah posted that she had baked focaccia, Madelyn Gosselin wrote, "slay omg."

While Leah might not have a relationship with her father Jon, she does seem to be on good terms with her mother Kate. In April 2023, she posted a TikTok of Kate blowing out candles on a pretty birthday cake. She wrote, "Lemon desserts always make me think of you!! Happy (belated) Birthday Mommy."

What Has Jon And Kate Plus 8 Star Leah Hope Gosselin Said About Her Family?

Leah Hope Gosselin doesn't seem to have been interviewed about her time on Jon And Kate Plus 8. She doesn't seem to have made any public comments about her family, either.

It's possible that Leah is quiet because she and her siblings have had to read too many mean comments that people have posted online.

In February 2023, Page Six reported that Madelyn Gosselin talked about her siblings on TikTok. She said, “This is the singular time I’m going to address this because it is sending me over the edge. The rhetoric in so many of my comments about childhood trauma and healing and whatever you want to say about my family, my life, my parents, whatever is not your business.”

Madelyn also praised her siblings, which suggests that they are fairly close. Madelyn wrote, “They are all amazing people. They are all smart, they are all kind, they’re driven students, they’re working hard, they’re funny, they’re stylish."

It's possible that Madelyn and Leah Hope Gosselin have a good relationship. It's sweet to see Madelyn commenting on Leah's TikTok and supporting her love of baking.

Leah and Hannah Gosselin also seem to have always shared a nice bond. In 2013, People reported on what Leah and Hannah, who were nine years old at the time, wanted to do when they were grown-ups. They both said that they were planning to open a bakery together. Leah said, "I want to teach little kids, and then I'll work in Hannah's bakery."

While Jon And Kate Plus 8 fans remain curious about what Leah Hope Gosselin is doing now and how she feels about her family, her life seems to be full of happiness. She has been through a lot with her family, and it's nice to see that she is enjoying college so much.
