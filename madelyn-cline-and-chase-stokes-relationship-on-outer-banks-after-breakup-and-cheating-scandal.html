Did Madelyn Cline And Chase Stokes Get Along On The Set Of Outer Banks Post-Break Up?

Since Madelyn Cline and Chase Stokes play Sarah and John B., two characters who fall in love with one another, it wasn't a huge surprise to learn that the actors were dating IRL. Sarah is part of the group called the Kooks and John B. runs the Pogues, which causes some tension. And, of course, these two groups make the show especially fun to watch since there's always something dramatic going on.

Did Cline and Stokes have trouble getting along when they had to film season 3 of Outer Banks? It turns out that they split up in the autumn months of 2021 and had to be on set in February 2022. For many people, this would have been impossible. Or at least pretty awkward.

The actors have remained totally polite with each other, however, and they seem to have put their personal life aside to focus on the characters that they love.

Cline and Stokes talked about filming Outer Banks with Entertainment Weekly in February 2023. Cline said, "We had a working relationship before we had a personal relationship, and our job from day one has always been to leave the show better than we found it."

Cline continued to Entertainment Weekly, "I'm really, really happy and I'm very proud of the work this season, and I'm incredibly grateful for my co-stars and their professionalism and just how talented each and every one of them are."

Chase Stokes shared with Entertainment Weekly that they didn't want their break-up to affect Outer Banks. And according to Stokes, they talked about this even prior to being in a romantic relationship.

Stokes told Entertainment Weekly, "Mads and I have been working together now for 30 episodes, and we made a promise to each other before we even started dating that the work was always going to come first. And that no matter what happens in our personal lives, and the ways that life sometimes takes you in different directions, that we're always going to honor the work. That stayed 100 percent truthful this season."

When asked about filming Outer Banks after the breakup, Cline told People, "at the end of the day, there's always been a safe space with the job." She said she was "happy that we are able to remain professional."
