Who Is Chris Buckner?

Deena Cortese was introduced to the Jersey Shore family back in season 3 when she was brought in as Angelina Pivarnick's replacement.

Between the two final seasons, a lot changed for Deena, who met Chris Buckner on the shore back in 2011. The duo had exchanged numbers and added one another on Facebook before officially starting to date.

After Buckner first appeared on the show come its final season, fans were excited for the star to finally have a romance she could obsess over! Well, as viewers got to see more of Chris, they began asking more and more questions.
