Nikki and Maryse start the match off. Maryse tags in Miz. Maryse doesn’t want any parts of Nikki. Miz gets on the top rope and jaws with the crowd. Cena charges at Miz. Miz rolls to the outside. Nikki dives through the ropes and takes out Miz. Back in the ring. Nikki elbows Maryse in the face. Cena hits a spinout slam on Miz. Double five knuckle shuffles by Cena and Nikki. Cena hits the AA on Miz, Nikki hits the Rack Attack 2.0 for the win.
