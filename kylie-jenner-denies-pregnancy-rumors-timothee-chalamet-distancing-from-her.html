Kylie Jenner Denies She Is Pregnant With Timothee Chalamet's Baby

As fans of socialite and beauty maven Kylie Jenner know, she once dated rapper and father of her two kids, Travis Scott for five years before they split.

After that time, Jenner primarily focused on her many businesses, modeling, and attending events. That is until she began to date actor Timothee Chalamet in April 2023.

While the romance surprised fans, it has since been revealed that the two had been seeing each other for several months before they made their relationship public.

Jenner and Chalamet made headlines when they attended the 2024 Golden Globe Awards in January 2024. During the show's airing, the two were caught by cameras coyly whispering to each other and sharing kisses, which quickly went viral on the internet.

Fast forward to April 2024, and social media rumors spread that Kylie Jenner's expecting her third baby with Timothee Chalamet.

That is not to say that Jenner has ruled out having another baby.

In an April 2023 interview with Homme Girls, Kylie Jenner was asked if she planned on having more children.

I don't have a number in my mind. Some women do, but I don't really have a plan. I think that whatever happens is meant to happen.
