    Big Career

    In 1988, Daddy released his first album on Cold Chillin’ Records called Long Live the Kane. He later released his second album and biggest hit to date, It’s a Big Daddy Thing, which included “Smooth Operator” and “I Get the Job Done”, which hit the R&B top 40 during the closing of the 1980s. Since 1980s until todate he has appeared in many mixtapes singles and albums. He also released the single “Enough”, which address issues about police brutality in 2020.
