One Direction Fans Think There Might Be A Connection Between 'Olivia' And Harry Styles' Romance

Harry Styles and director Olivia Wilde have been officially together for almost two years, and their relationship still seems to make waves for every little thing. Especially after the release of Olivia's movie, Don't Worry Darling, starring Harry Styles and Florence Pugh, the couple have been making headlines periodically.

One of the many things fans have focused on lately is the One Direction song 'Olivia'. The reason why people made the connection between the track and the director is obvious, but the truth is that there is no real reason to believe the song has anything to do with Olivia Wilde.

Harry Styles And Olivia Wilde's Somewhat Controversial Relationship

It was a bit of a shock for fans when the news of Harry Styles and Olivia Wilde's relationship came out, and even though they have been together for a while now, their romance still causes controversy sometimes.

The drama surrounding Olivia's new movie, Don't Worry Darling, and the director's very public custody battle didn't help, but even so, people tend to be overly harsh when judging their relationship.

Although the couple has chosen to remain private for the most part, both Olivia and Harry have expressed how annoyed they are. Particularly the singer said he doesn't like how people treat Olivia.

"That obviously doesn’t make me feel good," Harry said recently. "It’s obviously a difficult feeling to feel like being close to me means you’re at the ransom of a corner of Twitter or something. I just wanted to sing. I didn’t want to get into it if I was going to hurt people like that."

He also said he doesn't understand "that kind of toxic negativity" some fans direct towards his relationship, but he also said he doesn't think that represents his fandom. "The majority of them are true champions of kindness," he said.

For her part, Olivia is trying her best not to let the bad comments affect her, but admittedly, it's not always easy.

"It’s obviously really tempting to correct a false narrative," she explained. "But I think what you realise is that when you’re really happy it doesn’t matter what strangers think about you. All that matters to you is what’s real, and what you love, and who you love."

Why Fans Went Crazy About Harry Styles Singing 'Olivia'

Had Harry Styles released a song titled 'Olivia' in the past couple of years, the connection between the name of the song and his girlfriend would have been a no-brainer.

As things stand, however, him performing the One Direction song 'Olivia' caused a commotion in the fandom. The song is part of One Direction's last album, Made in the A.M., which came out in 2015, right before the band went on a hiatus that continues to this day.

The album featured only four members, because by that time Zayn Malik had left the group, and they parted ways before they even got to tour. This means the song 'Olivia' was never performed live by One Direction. So, it was a big surprise when Harry Styles performed it live in Las Vegas in 2021.

As always, fans came up with theories about why he chose to suddenly play that particular song. Those included the speculation that he already loved Olivia Wilde back then, though there is nothing that indicates that they had any sort of relationship before they started working together on Don't Worry Darling.

Nonetheless, a lot of people can't help connecting the director with the song since then.

The True Meaning Behind The Song

Regardless of whether or not Harry Styles had an ulterior motive to play that song on that particular night, he never could have expected people to react so strongly. And he definitely couldn't have predicted he'd be asked about the meaning of a song he wrote with his old band over five years ago.

But fortunately for him, he had already answered that question years ago, when the song first came out. So, in case readers were wondering what 'Olivia' is really about, this is what the singer shared.

"You can put names in things, and it seems like there’s no grey area, but it doesn’t have to be so literal," Harry explained.

"'Olivia' could be a place or a family member, it’s not necessarily about me. It might be an observation. Obviously when you listen to it, you assume that there’s a girl called Olivia. Which could be true, but isn’t necessarily the case. It’s cool to kind of explore — I always think it’s fun to take a situation or an emotion then personify it."

Harry Styles did, however, dedicate lots of his new songs to Olivia Wilde. The couple seems to be very happy together, despite all the negativity from the public, so there's no telling where they'll go together.
