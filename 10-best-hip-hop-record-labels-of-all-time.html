Hip-hop is one of the oldest music genres in American history, especially from the amazing celebrities in this industry. The following are the ten best hip-hop record labels of all time.

10. Suave House

Tony Draper started the label in the early 90's. 8Ball, Memphis duo, and MJG were also signed in year 1990. Other rappers available were South Circle, Tela, and Crime boss. However, in the year 2000 MJG and 8Ball left the label making it not receive any noticeable success. It sold a records amounting to $40 million.

9. Cash Money

This company was extremely successful although it is noted that some people did not particularly enjoy this label. Some of the top artists in this label are B.G., Lil Wayne, Drake, and Hot Boyz. It sold $87 million in records.

8. Cold Chillin'

They were responsible for several of the golden years of hip-hop rap in the late 80's and early 90's. These Albums were mainly by classics like Biz Markie, Big Daddy Kane, MC Shan, and Kool G. Rap. The company died however in 1998 after all members left. It sold$ 89 million worth of records.

7. Roc Nation

This was the early Jay-z label before he went and joined a different one. Alongside Jay-z, there was Dame as well as Kareem Burke. It sold $ 80 million of records.

6. Ruff Ryders

The Dean Brothers founded this hip-hop label in 90's in New York hip-hop. Some of the rappers include The L.O.X, DMX, and Swizz Beatz. Nevertheless, this hip-hop label has died since recently as numerous rappers have moved to other labels. It sold $ 160 million worth of records.

5. Bad Boy

The artists like Puff Daddy, Notorious BIG, and the Family used this label. They were all legends in the process of making when this great label was on the market. What a great label. It sold $ 130 million in records.

4. Def Jam

This label had some of the hip-hop legends such as  the Beastie Boys, Public Enemy, Nas, , DMX, LL Cool J, Ja Rule, and Jay-Z, alongside the modern day artists like Young Jeezy, Jam people, Pusha T, Kanye West, and Rick Ross. This is definitely one of the biggest as well as the best labels in the hip-hop history. It sold records for $150 million.

3. Shady Records

This was started by Paul Rosenberg and Eminem in the year 1999 and has borrowed a lot of music partnership from Aftermath. Therefore, along with the way some of the Aftermath artists were coerced to join the shady records. Some of them were house, Obie Trice, Yelawolf, and Bad Meets Evil.

2. Aftermath

This is one of the best beats ever produced hip-hop label. Aftermath studios were started by legend star Dr. Dre in the year 1996. The artists include 50 Cent, Dr. Dre, Kendrick Lamar, Eminem, and others. Other featured artists were Stat Quo, Busta Rhymes, and The Game.

1. Death Row

This label was founded in the year 1991 by the popular Suge Knight jr. This record sold approximately for over $50 million worldwide. Albums like the Doggystyle, all Eyez on me,  and Chronic where all amazing. Artists included Snoop Dogg ,Dr. Dre, Daz, Tupac, The Dogg Pound, Mc Hammer, Nate Dogg, and West Coast Baby.
