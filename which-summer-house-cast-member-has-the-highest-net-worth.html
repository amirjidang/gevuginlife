The Richest 'Summer House' Cast Member

Bravo knows what they are doing when it comes to reality television programming! With their success from shows such as 'The Real Housewives', 'Vanderpump Rules', and 'Below Deck', it was only fitting for the hit network to come out with more reality TV gold and they certainly delivered. 'Summer House' originally aired in 2017 and is currently set to air its fifth season come February 2021. While the original cast remains viewers' favorite, the newest castmates have definitely brought the heat, making for great additions to the show.

The group rents a home every summer in Montauk, New York, which is currently on the market for $5 million, where they escape the busy city every weekend to let loose and get into a lot of trouble! Considering the allure that comes with living in New York City and summer-ing in Montauk, fans are wondering just how wealthy the cast is and more specifically, who is the richest? Well, believe it or not, but the only original cast members left on the show, Carl Radke, Kyle Cooke, and Lindsay Hubbard all come in with a net worth of $1.1 million!

It goes without saying that the trio does quite well for themselves outside of the show, but just how much are they racking in each season?

Well, while their salaries aren't disclosed, many fans believe that the three cast members are making anywhere between $10,000 - $20,000 per episode, which is on par with the cast of 'Vanderpump Rules'. With their salaries from Bravo and their real 9 to 5's, it is no surprise that Carl, Kyle, and Lindsay have all been able to amass a little over $1 million throughout their careers in business and reality television!
