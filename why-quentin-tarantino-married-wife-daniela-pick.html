What Made Quentin Tarantino Settle Down With His Wife Daniella Pick?

During the first several years of Quentin Taratino’s relationship with Daniella Pick, it didn’t seem like he was ever going to change his ways by walking down the aisle. After all, Tarantino and Pick dated each other without getting engaged from 2009 until 2017. On top of that, the same year that he began dating Pick, Tarantino said something to GQ that made it seem like he prioritized making movies over dating or being a father.

"When I'm doing a movie, I'm not doing anything else. It's all about the movie. I don't have a wife. I don't have a kid. Nothing can get in my way. The whole (expletive) world can go to hell and burst into flames. I don't care. This is my life."

"I'm not saying that I'll never get married or have a kid before I'm 60. But I've made a choice, so far, to go on this road alone. Because this is my time. This is my time to make movies."

As it turns out, Quentin Tarantino’s 2009 comments about marriage were a harbinger of things to come. After all, it wasn’t until 2017 that he would get engaged. On top of that, Tarantino went on to get married in 2018 and in recent years, he has been talking about the prospect of retiring from making movies a lot more.
