The Khadra Sisters' Early Life: From Saudi To The US

She has an identical twin sister named Haze Khadra. Simi and Haze’s mother ran a successful fashion boutique in Riyadh. At just 14, they tagged along with their mother to Paris Fashion Week, which gave the twin sisters early exposure to the fashion world, as per Elle.
