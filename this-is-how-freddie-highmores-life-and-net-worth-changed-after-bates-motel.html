This Is How Freddie Highmore's Life And Net Worth Changed After 'Bates Motel'

August 13, 2024
post

29-year old English actor Freddie Highmore started his career in 1999 by holding a role in the comedy movie Women Talking Dirty. Earlier in his working years, he participated in several movies, including Two Brothers, Finding Neverland, Five Children And It, Charlie And The Chocolate Factory, August Rush, Astro Boy, and The Art Of Getting By. Freddie also starred in several TV series such as The Mists Of Avalon and Jack and The Beanstalk: The Real Story, and in TV films namely Happy Birthday Shakespeare, I Saw You, and Toast.

Freddie Highmore is famously known for his role as Norman Bates in the American drama-thriller series Bates Motel that aired on A&E from 2013 to 2017. The actor even moved on to writing and directing some of the show's episodes. There’s no doubt that Freddie Highmore’s life has significantly changed after Bates Motel ended. Let’s explore the most recent changes in the life of the Bates Motel star.

9 Freddie Highmore Married A Wonderful Woman

In response to a question by Jimmy Kimmel about the wedding ring he is wearing, Freddie confirmed during the Jimmy Kimmel Live! episode that he got married. Freddie didn’t reveal any details about the identity of his wife besides that she’s British. He described her as a "very wonderful" woman. The media speculated that the Bates Motel star’s wife might be Sarah Bolger, the actress from Spiderwick Chronicles. They also questioned whether Highmore’s wife is Dakota Fanning, with whom Freddie went on a dinner date in 2009.

8 He Accumulated $8 Million In Wealth

Thanks to his dedicated work and successful career, Freddie Highmore amassed a fortune amounting in value to $8 million. At 29-years-old, Freddie has plenty of time to increase his wealth to a high maximum. Since 1999, Highmore has starred in more than 21 movies and 12 tv films and series. He also had roles in five video games and two music videos. Moreover, Freddie is a writer and producer for several series as well.

7 He Was Cast As The Lead Role For ABC’s 'The Good Doctor'

In 2017, Freddie Highmore was offered his most prominent lead role when he starred in ABC’s American medical drama TV series The Good Doctor. Freddie participated as the young autistic savant doctor Sean Murphy. The series follows the story of surgeon Dr. Murphy who was born in Wyoming and moved to San Francisco, California, to work at a well-known hospital.

6 'The Good Doctor' Season 5 Premiered With Freddie As The Lead Role, Again

On September 27th, the 5th season of the ABC series The Good Doctor returned. In this season, Shaun will get married to Lea, played by Paige Spara. However, the show's trailer reveals some problems related to wedding planning and new patient lives that need to be saved.

5 Highmore Starred In 'The Vault'

Freddie starred in 2021 in a Spanish action thriller movie called The Vault, known as Way Down in other regions. Highmore plays the lead role of Thom in the show. Thom is studying engineering at Cambridge and is a genius student on the quest of breaking into the safe of the bank of Spain.

4 Freddie Signed With ICM Partners In All Areas

In April 2021, Freddie Highmore revealed that he signed with ICM Partners in all areas. Freddie has been starring as Shaun Murphy for five consecutive seasons in the ABC series The Good Doctor since 2017. He owns a production company called Alfresco Pictures that partnered with Sony Pictures to produce and broadcast productions, such as the series Leonardo.

3 Highmore Continues To Hide From Social Media

Although The Good Doctor’s Freddie Highmore has verified social media accounts on both Instagram and Twitter, the star doesn’t post and doesn't follow anyone. Freddie justified his decision not to use social media by claiming he wanted to keep a clear difference between his personal and professional life. He also said it helped him stay psychologically grounded.

2 He Is Grateful For His Good Health

Freddie declared that he feels blessed that he stayed healthy during the Covid-19 pandemic. He said that these times taught him to be grateful for the blessings he may not have noticed before. Freddie paid tribute in season 4 of The Good Doctor to the health professionals that gave it all during the pandemic.

1 Freddie Was Nominated For Several Awards

Highmore was nominated for a handful of awards recently. One of them is the 2018 Golden Globe Award for Best Actor- Television Series Drama for his role as Shaun Murphy in The Good Doctor. He was also nominated twice for the Critics’ Choice TV Awards in 2019 and 2020 for the same show role. The same was for the 2018 Teen Choice Awards for Best TV Actor - Drama and the 2018 Gold Derby Awards for Best Drama Actor. The actor has won 12 awards and received 44 award nominations during his career years.
