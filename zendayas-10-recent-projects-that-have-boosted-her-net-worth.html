Zendaya proved to be a terrific performer from a young age and was a part of a dance troupe before she acted in stage productions. After being cast in the Disney comedy show Shake It Up, she gained popularity, which aired for three seasons, made music while pursuing her acting career, and released her debut album, Zendaya, in 2013. After starring in a drama series for Disney until 2018, the artist received her big break in Hollywood when she entered the Marvel Cinematic Universe. While the actress had a net worth of $5 million until 2019, her various ventures in Hollywood helped her boost to a whopping $15 million.

From getting a steady start with a hit franchise to slowly showing the world her talent as a dramatic actress and designer, she has been one of the most talked-about celebrities in the past few years.

Spider-Man Franchise

While Zendaya had previously worked on Disney shows, her breakthrough role on the screen came in 2017 when she starred as MJ in Spider-Man: Homecoming. As reported by Mirror, the actress made $300,000 from the first movie while she was paid $2 million for the second installment, Spider-Man: Far From Home. For the third movie, Spider-Man: No Way Home, she made $2 million for reprising the role. The three movies have collectively grossed over $1.4 billion worldwide.

Dune

An epic film by Dennis Villeneuve, Dune is set in the far future and follows the story of Paul Atreides portrayed by Timothée Chalamet. Zendaya plays the supporting character of a mysterious Freman named Chani. The movie was a box office success, and the actress made $300,000 for her role. Due to its popularity, the movie already has a sequel in the works for a 2023 release.

Malcolm And Marie

A black and white movie made during the quarantine, Malcolm And Marie tells the story of a stormy night between a couple played by John David Washington and Z. The actress, along with her co-star, gave shares of the movie to the skeleton crew. When the movie sold to Netflix for $30 million, each person made a minimum of $300,000 from the sale.

Euphoria

Euphoria is one of Zendaya’s most popular projects as the actress has enhanced her acting abilities in the drama series playing the character of Ruby or Rue Bennett, a teenage drug addict. The actress reportedly earned upwards of $50,000 per episode for her starring role as the supporting cast made $25,000 per episode, as stated by Parade. In 2020, she became the youngest recipient to win an Emmy for Actress in a Leading Role, Drama.

Space Jam: A New Legacy

In her first voice acting role, she starred in the 2021 family-comedy live-action animated movie Space Jam: A New Legacy. The actress voiced the character of Lola Bunny as she was a big fan of screenwriter Coogler and the earlier version of the movie that starred Michael Jordan. The new movie saw Lebron James at the helm, and it grossed $162.8 million at the box office worldwide.

Tommy Hilfiger X Zendaya

Zendaya has become one of the biggest fashion icons with her red carpet looks, and she took her passion to the runway when she collaborated with Tommy Hilfiger for the Fall 2019 campaign. As mentioned by Vogue, the collection was a tribute to African American history and was detailed with the themes of the 70s fashion era. She worked alongside her stylist Law Roach to bring her collection to life.

Brand Ambassador For Lancôme

A leading luxury beauty brand, the star has been the face of Lancôme since September 2019, when she debuted the Idôle fragrance for the brand. Over the last three years, the actress has starred in campaigns and commercials for the perfume line and joined stars like Taylor Hill to become the face of the L'Oréal-owned company.

Valentino Brand Campaign

In December 2020, Zendaya became the global ambassador for fashion house Valentino. The Italian company showcased her bold yet elegant personality as she starred in the 2021 Fall campaign and advertised the clothing, shoes, and accessories line, which gained a lot of attention on the internet.

Face Of Bulgari

The artist bagged the spot as the new face of Bulgari Jewelry in February 2020 has been spotted wearing the brand’s jewelry for her red carpet outfits. According to Elle, her spirit as a popular figure with Generation Z has helped the brand tap the youth and the global market to give a universal appeal. In 2021, Zendaya starred in the Magnifica campaign for Bulgari.

Super Bowl Commercial For Squarespace

Squarespace tapped the right celebrity to advertise its services as a website building and hosting company. Starring in a 1-minute TV spot that reportedly cost upwards of $12 million, the actress starred as Sally, an entrepreneur who sells seashells on the seashore, a concept adopted from a century-old tongue twister.

From starring in blockbuster movies and award-winning television shows to expanding her interests in fashion and brand endorsements, Zendaya has seen an astronomical jump in her net worth since 2019. Actively working in movies and television, the actress is currently starring in the second season of Euphoria and will be next seen in the sequel Dune: Part II in 2023.
