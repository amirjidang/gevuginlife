Michael Jordan has been a basketball legend since the 1990s. He was the powerhouse behind the Chicago Bulls team. He became a pop culture phenomenon, starring in the movie Space Jam, having his own shoe (the “Air Jordan”), and excelling at the sport of baseball.

Although Michael’s professional life is very well-known, his personal life has been somewhat shrouded. Yes, he’s been accused of having a gambling problem (which was later proved to be unfounded criticism), and the whole world knew when Michael’s father was brutally murdered. However, there’s not much more that fans know...especially regarding his love life.

Michael’s first wife, Juanita Vanoy, gave him three celeb offspring - yes, we mean three beautiful children, but Michael and Juanita's relationship ended in 2006. After splitting from Juanita, Michael met Yvette Prieto, a former model living in Miami, Florida.

Michael and Yvette are now married but keep their relationship super-private. Here’s everything we could find on Michael’s latest lady!

15 Cuban-Born

Yvette Prieto was born in Cuba but grew up in Miami, Florida, which is the perfect place to celebrate her Cuban heritage! In Miami, she worked as a model for designers like Alexander Wang. Given Yvette’s shorter stature (she is 5’ 6”), she probably did a lot of print jobs, as opposed to runway work.

14 Famous Ex

Apparently, Yvette is used to the spotlight! Before Michael, she dated Enrique Iglesias’s brother, Julio Iglesias, Jr. Julio certainly isn’t as famous as his brother, but he did find some success with music and television work that was geared toward the Latin community. Being a model at the time, Yvette was the perfect companion.

13 Love In The Club

In 2007, just one year after divorcing Juanito Vanoy, Michael met Yvette while visiting a Miami nightclub. Given how they met, it’s obvious that Michael was initially attracted to Yvette’s outer beauty. However, considering how long they’ve lasted (13 years), he’s someone who appreciates her incredible character, as well.

12 Took Things Slow

Having already divorced once, Michael was careful not to rush into another relationship. Therefore, Michael and Yvette started dating in 2007 but didn’t move in together until 2009. From there, they were engaged in 2011 and married in 2013. The two experienced six full years together before tying the knot!

11 Almost-Royal Wedding

Michael and Yvette’s 2013 wedding was super-lavish (of course - Michael is basketball royalty!) The Palm Beach wedding ceremony featured a beautifully decorated, 40,000 square-foot tent for dancing, food, and fun. Moreover, Robin Thicke and Usher were wedding singers!

When Michael and Yvette wed in 2013, Yvette was 34 years old and Michael was her first husband. So, Yvette was second-in-line to Juanita Vanoy - Michael’s first wife and the mother of three of his children. Luckily for Yvette, Michael and Juanita are pretty chill co-parents. They don’t have much drama.

One can only hope that her history of dating wealthy men isn’t based on some desperate money situation. One thing's for sure - she's not hurting for cash now.
